We are MIRROR

Together we reflect unlimited possibilities

A Hong Kong Canto-pop band…

  • Formed by 12 unique boys, all filled with great passion for performance.
  • Strived through a 6-month journey in ViuTV’s reality talent show “Good Night Show – King Maker” (literally “star maker” in Cantonese) with passion and persistence, competing against 99 shortlisted young male talents in front of all Hong Kong audience.
  • Named MIRROR, because mirror could reflect the most authentic face of each member, just like how they showcased themselves in the reality show. At the same time, each unique member is like an individual mirror. When 12 mirrors are put together, they could reflect infinite possibilities.
  • Debuted on November 3, 2018 with their first single – “In a Second”. Captured a number of awards, including Fairchild Radio Canadian Chinese Pop Music Award’s “Best New Groups” in 2019 and “Best Group” in 2020.
  • Believes in the revival of the Canto-pop culture. Conveys a new form of Hong Kong spirit of “perseverance and solidarity” amid the COVID-19 pandemic.
